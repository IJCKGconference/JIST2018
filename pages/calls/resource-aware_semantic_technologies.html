Introduction

Joint International Semantic Technology Conference (JIST 2018)

As Semantic Technologies gain momentum, their impact on resources and society increases. At the same time application developers would like to employ semantic technologies on devices that are heavily resource constrained such as mobile devices. So far the efficiency of semantic technologies is still mainly assessed by running times. While computational and cognitive complexity are investigated, work on their resource consumption, e.g. in terms of energy or storage, is still in its infancy. However, assessing, managing and predicting the resource-consumption is a vital prerequisite for the wider usefulness of semantic technologies.

The special session calls for contributions regarding, but not limited to the following topics:

  • - resource-adaptive semantic technologies
  • - reasoning algorithms for resource-bound hardware
  • - measurement frameworks for energy-consumption of semantic technologies
  • - ontologies for energy management
  • - prediction methods for resource consumption
  • - application of semantic technologies for resource management
