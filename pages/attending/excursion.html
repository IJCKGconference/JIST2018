We will have two options for excursion on 28 Nov afternoon.

1. Naruto Whirlpools Cruise

The Naruto Whirlpools are tidal whirlpools between Awaji Island and Shikoku Island. You can see the whirlpools near by taking a cruise.

Tentative Schedule:

  • 14:10 Leaving the venue by bus
  • 15:10 Boarding point of boat
  • 16:10-17:00 Cruise
  • After the cruise, going to Sannomiya Station via the venue

2. Japanese Sake Brewery

The Hyogo prefecture is one of most famous area for the brewing Japanese sake (rice wine). In Awaji Islands, there are some sake brewery. You can visit one of the sake brewery, and taste some of the products.

Tentative Schedule:

  • 14:20 Leaving the venue by bus
  • 14:30-16:00 Visit of Japanese sake brewery
  • After the visit, going to Sannomiya Station via the venue
