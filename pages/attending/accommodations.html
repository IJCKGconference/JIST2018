Hotel Reservation

Hotel reservation via JIST 2018 site is enabled with the conference registration or after the conference registration. Note that you can not make a reservation for a room of HOTEL Ebisu from the registration site. To book a room of of HOTEL Ebisu, please email to jist2018-reservationknowledge-graph.jp with the following information:

  • Your Name
  • Log-in ID for the Registration site
  • Dates of Check-in and Check-out
  • Room Type (Single or Twin for single use)
  • Breakfast Plan (Breakfast Included or Without Breakfast)
