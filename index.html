JIST2018: The 8th Joint International Semantic Technology Conference Nov 26 - 28, 2018, Awaji City, Hyogo, Japan

The Joint International Semantic Technology Conference (JIST) is a joint event for regional Semantic related conferences.
The JIST2018 will take place from November 26 to 28, 2018 in the Awaji Yumebutai International Conference Center, Awaji City, Hyogo, Japan.

Organizers

- JSAI-SIG on Semantic Web and Ontology
- JIST Steering Committee
